Registration

The registration fee is 170 EUR for EYE members and 200 EUR for non-EYE members.

It covers:

  • • 3 hotel nights in shared room, including breakfast
  • • Conference material
  • • Lunches and dinners, including gala dinner
  • • Transportation to industrial visits

Deadlines

  • • 24th January 2020 – Registration
  • • 31st January 2020 – Payment of the fee
  • • 2nd March 2020 – Final Programme
  • • 7th - 10th May 2020 – Days of the Conference

The conference has limited number of participants, so the rule “First Come First Serve” will be applied.

If the maximum capacity is reached the registration will be closed.
