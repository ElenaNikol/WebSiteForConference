European Young Engineers Conference

BRIDGING ENGINEERING PERSPECTIVES

7-10 May, Ohrid

About the conference

46th European Young Engineers Conference BRIDGING ENGINEERING PERSPECTIVES is held in the Republic of North Macedonia for the first time and has the goal of bringing young engineers from different associations in Europe in order to share knowledge and develop personal and professional skills.

BRIDGING ENGINEERING PERSPECTIVES is the topic of this unique conference that combines inspiring keynotes, panels, workshops, industrial visits and insight into how to navigate through the industry challenges. CEOs, university professors, experts and professionals within several industries will gather together to network, learn about new trends, and listen to world-class speakers and best practices.

Come and join us from 7th to 10th of May in Ohrid, the city of lights, where the First Slavic University is established in ninth century at 886.
